About TigerJobs

TigerJobs helps Princeton students find their next internship, job, or career with the help of their peers. With our app, you can search through job and interview reviews left by fellow Princeton students to discover new employment options. The jobs and interviews pages also allow you to filter through these reviews to find opportunities that fit your criteria or to explore what students similar to you in class year and major have done. Plus, every review links to a company or program with its own page to help you find other reviews and company stats. We hope you enjoy our site!


By using this website, you agree that you will not disclose information in violation of any legally enforceable confidentiality, non-disclosure or other contractual restrictions or rights of any third party. In addition, note that reviews of Princeton sponsored experiences may reflect biases in ratings.
