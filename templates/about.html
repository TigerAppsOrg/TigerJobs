About TigerJobs

TigerJobs helps Princeton students find their next internship, job, or career with the help of their peers. With TigerJobs, you can search through job and interview reviews left by fellow Princeton students to discover new employment options. In addition, you can filter through these reviews to find opportunities that fit your criteria or to explore what students similar to you in class year and major have done. If you're more interested in finding popular opportunities, check out the companies page, where you can find the most common companies that Princeton students have worked for. Make sure to add your own experiences in on the Profile page to help the next job-seekers find their perfect career as well. We hope you enjoy our site!
