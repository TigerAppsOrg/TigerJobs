Help Page

On the Jobs/Interviews page, you can search and filter through different reviews left by fellow students. The results update automatically as you type/select filters. You can also sort the reviews based on the selected criteria. To better understand the reviews, check out the rating scales which describe what the numbers in the reviews mean. If you see any reviews that you want to save, make sure to upvote them. If you see a review that has inappropriate content, make sure to report it so we can remove it. You can click on the company name of any review to be taken to a page showing all reviews and stats for that company. To add your own review, click on the "Add your own review!" button, which will take you to the Profile page.



On the Profile page, first fill in your major, certificates, and class year and then press update. You won't be able to submit a review without doing so. Next press leave a review and then select the type of review you want to leave. Any questions with an asterisk are required while the rest are optional. Note that the only info that will be shown is the info you submit, and your major/certificates. Be careful not to put any information in violation of a confidentiality agreement. After submitting a review, you can see all the reviews you've left in the section below.



If you want to edit a review you submitted, go to the "Your reviews" section and click on the review you want to change. Then, you can edit or delete the review.



On the Companies page, you can search through popular companies among Princeton students of different majors. You can either search for a company by name (again, this will update as you type), or see the top companies by major. The top companies are simply the companies with the most total reviews from the given major, so this is a good way to find companies that people from your major consistently apply to. By clicking on the company name, you'll be taken to a page with more details about the company, including other reviews and company stats.



For a specific company, you can see details such as locations people have worked at, fields offered, and majors of students who have applied or worked there. You can also see statistics about how people felt about working and interviewing at the company. For more specific info, all reviews for the company are on the right hand side. By default, both job and interview reviews show up. To filter by only job reviews, click "reviews" in the Jobs at Company section. To filter by only interview reviews, click "reviews" in the Interviews at Company section. To show all reviews, click "total reviews". The tooltips give the same information in case you forget.
